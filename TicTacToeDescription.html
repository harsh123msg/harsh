Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a classic two-player game played on a 3x3 grid. The game involves players taking turns to place their respective symbols, usually "X" and "O", in empty squares within the grid. The objective of the game is to be the first player to form a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of their symbols.

Successful play requires strategic placement of symbols to either achieve a winning configuration or to block the opponent from doing so. Tic Tac Toe is simple yet engaging, making it a popular choice for casual gaming and educational purposes.
